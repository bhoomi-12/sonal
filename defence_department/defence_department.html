About the Department of Defense

The Department of Defense is mandated with the defense policy of India and every part thereof. It deals with Inter-Service
Organisations, Defense Accounts Department, Canteen Stores Department (CSD), Coast Guard, National Cadet Corps, Border
Road Organisation, Institute of Defense Studies and Analyses, National Defense College etc. It is responsible for the defense
budget, defense lands and cantonments, matters relating to Parliament, and defense cooperation with foreign countries. It is
headed by the Defense Secretary who is assisted by Director General (Acquisition), Additional Secretaries and Joint Secretaries.
The Defense Secretary is also responsible for coordinating the activities of other departments in the Ministry of Defense viz.
DMA, DDP, DESW and DDR&D.
